Maxfiylik siyosati — Piknic UZ Instagram assistenti

Qanday ma'lumot yig'iladi

  • Instagram Direct xabarlarida siz yozgan matn (mahsulotga oid savol-javoblar)
  • Siz o'zingiz DM orqali bergan ism va telefon raqamingiz
  • Video/post ostiga qoldirgan kommentingiz matni

Ma'lumot qanday ishlatiladi

Yig'ilgan ma'lumotlar faqat quyidagi maqsadlarda ishlatiladi:

  • Sizga mahsulot haqida ma'lumot berish va savollaringizga javob berish
  • Buyurtmangizni CRM tizimimizga (AmoCRM) kiritish va operatorimiz siz bilan bog'lanishi uchun

Ma'lumotlarni saqlash

Suhbat tarixi xizmat ko'rsatish sifatini ta'minlash uchun cheklangan muddatga saqlanadi. Ism va telefon raqamingiz CRM tizimimizda buyurtmangizni bajarish maqsadida saqlanadi.

Uchinchi tomonlar bilan ulashish

Ma'lumotlaringiz reklama maqsadida uchinchi tomonlarga sotilmaydi yoki berilmaydi. Faqat buyurtmani bajarish uchun ichki CRM va bildirishnoma tizimlarimizda ishlatiladi.

Ma'lumotlarni o'chirish

O'zingiz haqingizdagi ma'lumotlarni (suhbat tarixi, ism, telefon raqam) o'chirishni so'rash uchun Instagram orqali @piknic_uz akkountiga yoki t.me/piknic_uz orqali "Ma'lumotlarimni o'chiring" deb yozing. So'rovingiz 7 kun ichida bajariladi.

Privacy Policy — Piknic UZ Instagram Assistant

This page explains what information is collected and how it is used by the automated Instagram Direct/comment assistant operated by Piknic UZ (Instagram: @piknic_uz).

  • Text you send via Instagram Direct messages (product-related questions and answers)

  • The text of comments you leave under our videos/posts

How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with product information and answer your questions
  • To record your order in our CRM system (AmoCRM) so our operator can contact you

Conversation history is retained for a limited period to maintain service quality. Your name and phone number are stored in our CRM system for the purpose of fulfilling your order.

Sharing With Third Parties

Your information is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes. It is used solely within our internal CRM and notification systems to fulfill your order.

To request deletion of your data (conversation history, name, phone number), message @piknic_uz on Instagram or t.me/piknic_uz and write "Delete my data". Your request will be processed within 7 days.
